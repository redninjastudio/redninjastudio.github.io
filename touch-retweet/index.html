Touch Retweet

In the middle of a twitter campaign? Retweeting is Bored? So lazy to retweet your friends tweets and retweets? Then this is your app. You can retweet easily, with the minimum action required by any app, just touch the tweet to retweet and hold to mark it as favorite.
